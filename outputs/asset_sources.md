# Asset Sources and License Checklist

This file must be completed after the final video is edited. Do not list an asset as cleared unless its source and usage right have been confirmed.

## Policy

- Use self-made footage, self-made graphics, course-allowed materials, or openly licensed assets only.
- Do not use copyrighted music, images, videos, fonts, templates, school logos, department logos, or icons without permission.
- If a recognizable person appears, record consent before publishing the video.
- If official department information is quoted, cite the official source and date checked.
